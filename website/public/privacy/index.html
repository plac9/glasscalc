PrismCalc Privacy Policy

Clear, human-friendly privacy details for PrismCalc on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch.

Effective date: December 26, 2025
Company: LaClair Technologies

Overview

PrismCalc is designed to be private by default. We do not collect analytics, we do not run ads, and we do not track you across apps or websites. Your calculations stay on your device unless you choose to enable iCloud sync.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data, usage analytics, or device identifiers.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, PrismCalc stores your calculation history and preferences in iCloud so they can sync across your Apple devices. This data is handled by Apple and associated with your Apple ID.

  • Sync is optional and not required to use the app.
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in device settings.
  • We do not have access to your Apple ID credentials.

Sign in with Apple

Sign in with Apple is offered only to support iCloud sync. It is optional and not required for core functionality. We do not receive your Apple ID password or access your Apple ID account.

Third-Party Services

PrismCalc relies on Apple services for iCloud sync and authentication. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

PrismCalc does not knowingly collect personal data from children. The app functions without requiring personal information.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and the effective date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at info@prismcalc.app.
